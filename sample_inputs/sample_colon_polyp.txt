SURGICAL PATHOLOGY REPORT

Specimen: Colon, sigmoid, polypectomy.

Clinical history: Screening colonoscopy. 1.2 cm polyp in the sigmoid colon.

Gross description: Received in formalin is a tan-pink polypoid fragment of tissue measuring 1.2 x 0.9 x 0.6 cm. The specimen is bisected and entirely submitted.

Microscopic description: Sections show colonic mucosa with a tubular adenoma exhibiting low-grade dysplasia. There is no evidence of high-grade dysplasia, carcinoma in situ, or invasive carcinoma. The stalk margin is free of adenomatous tissue.

Diagnosis: Sigmoid colon, polypectomy - tubular adenoma with low-grade dysplasia. Margins clear.

Comment: Complete excision. Routine surveillance colonoscopy is recommended per guidelines.
